Ambient Asleep Technical Support Documentation

I. Overview

This technical support documentation is designed to provide users with help on the installation, use, and troubleshooting of "Ambient Asleep". If you encounter any problems while using the app, please refer to this documentation or contact our technical support team.

II. App Introduction

"Ambient Asleep" is an app dedicated to providing sleep-aiding audio. It has a rich variety of sleep-aiding audio resources, aiming to help users relax and enter a peaceful sleep state.

Main functions:

  • Play sleep-aiding audio: Provide a variety of high-quality sleep-aiding audio, including natural sounds, soothing music, etc.
  • Background play: Allow users to continue playing sleep-aiding audio when using other apps or when the device is locked.

III. System Requirements

1. Operating system: iOS 15.6 and above.

2. Device requirements: iPhone, iPad, iPod touch.

IV. Installation and Startup

1. Download and installation:

Search for "Ambient Asleep" in the App Store and click the download button to install it.

Ensure that your device has enough storage space to install the app.

2. Start the app:

After installation, find the app icon on the home screen and click to start it.

If it is the first startup, you may need to agree to some permission requests, such as accessing the microphone (for background play control). Please follow the prompts.

V. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Audio cannot be played:

Check whether the volume of your device is turned on and not set to silent mode.

Ensure that the audio permission of the app is allowed. You can find "Ambient Asleep" in the device settings and check the audio permission settings.

Try restarting the app or the device to see if the problem is resolved.

2. Background play does not work:

Confirm that your device settings allow the app to run in the background. You can find "General" > "Background App Refresh" in the device settings and ensure that "Ambient Asleep" has background refresh enabled.

If you are using low power mode, it may affect the background play function. Please turn off low power mode and try again.

VI. Troubleshooting

1. General troubleshooting steps:

Restart the device: Sometimes, a simple device restart can solve many problems.

Clear the app cache (if the app has this function): Find the app in the device settings and select the "Clear Cache" option. This can help solve some performance issues.

Update the app: Ensure that your app is the latest version to obtain the best performance and stability.

2. Collect error information:

If an error or abnormal situation occurs in the app, you can try to collect error information so that the technical support team can better help you solve the problem. Here are some methods:

  • Screenshot: If the error message appears on the screen, take a screenshot and save it.
  • Describe the specific situation and steps of the problem occurrence, including what operations were being performed when the problem occurred.

3. Use diagnostic tools (if the app has this function):

If the app provides diagnostic tools or debug modes, you can try to use them to help troubleshoot problems. These tools usually can provide more detailed error information and performance data.
